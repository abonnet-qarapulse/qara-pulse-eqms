# Quality Manual

## Purpose

The Quality Manual provides an overview of the Quality Management System (QMS), its structure, scope, and key principles. It serves as the top-level document that introduces the QMS framework and explains how all QMS components work together.

This document is intended to:
- Present the QMS scope and boundaries
- Describe the QMS structure and document hierarchy
- Explain the relationship between processes, procedures, and records
- Provide references to key QMS documents
- Demonstrate management commitment to quality
