# Quality Objectives

## Purpose

Quality Objectives define measurable goals that support the Quality Policy and drive continuous improvement of the Quality Management System. They provide concrete targets for quality performance and enable monitoring of QMS effectiveness.

This document is intended to:
- Establish measurable quality goals aligned with the Quality Policy
- Provide targets for quality performance monitoring
- Support management review and decision-making
- Enable tracking of QMS effectiveness and continuous improvement
- Demonstrate compliance with ISO 13485 requirements
