# Quality Policy

## Purpose

The Quality Policy establishes the organization's commitment to quality and serves as the foundation for the Quality Management System. It defines the organization's quality objectives and provides direction for all quality-related activities.

This document is intended to:
- Express top management's commitment to quality
- Define quality principles and objectives
- Guide decision-making and resource allocation
- Communicate quality expectations to all stakeholders
- Support regulatory compliance (ISO 13485 requirement)
