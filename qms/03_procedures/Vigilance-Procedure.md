# Vigilance Procedure

## 1. Introduction
### 1.1 Purpose
This procedure defines the requirements and process for reporting serious incidents and field safety corrective actions to competent authorities in accordance with MDR vigilance requirements.


### 1.2 Scope
This procedure applies to vigilance activities, including serious incident reporting, field safety corrective actions (FSCA), and periodic summary reports. Post-market surveillance activities are covered by the Post-Market Surveillance Procedure.
