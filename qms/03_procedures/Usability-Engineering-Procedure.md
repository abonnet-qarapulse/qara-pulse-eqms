# Usability Engineering Procedure

## 1. Introduction
### 1.1 Purpose
This procedure defines the requirements and process for usability engineering activities to ensure medical devices are safe and effective for use by intended users.


### 1.2 Scope
This procedure applies to usability engineering activities throughout the product lifecycle, including formative and summative usability evaluations. This procedure covers usability planning, evaluation, and documentation in accordance with IEC 62366-1.
