# Product Risk Management Procedure

## 1. Introduction
### 1.1 Purpose
This procedure defines the requirements and process for managing risks associated with medical devices throughout the product lifecycle, in accordance with ISO 14971.


### 1.2 Scope
This procedure applies to risk management of medical devices, including risk analysis, evaluation, and control. This procedure covers risk management files, risk management plans, and risk management reports. Process risks are covered by the Process Risk Management Procedure.
