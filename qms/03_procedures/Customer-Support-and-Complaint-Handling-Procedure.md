# Customer Support and Complaint Handling Procedure

## 1. Introduction
### 1.1 Purpose
This procedure defines the process for receiving, evaluating, investigating, and responding to customer complaints and support requests.


### 1.2 Scope
This procedure applies to all customer complaints, support requests, and feedback related to medical devices. Complaints that may qualify as serious incidents are handled according to the Vigilance Procedure.
