# Purchasing and Supplier Evaluation Procedure

## 1. Introduction
### 1.1 Purpose
This procedure defines the requirements and process for evaluating, selecting, and monitoring suppliers and purchased products/services.


### 1.2 Scope
This procedure applies to all purchasing activities that affect product quality or QMS conformity. This procedure covers supplier evaluation, qualification, monitoring, and control. Supplier audits are covered by the Audit Management Procedure.
