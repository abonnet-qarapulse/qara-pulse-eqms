# Audit Management Procedure

## 1. Introduction
### 1.1 Purpose
This procedure defines the requirements and activities for planning, conducting, and managing internal and external audits of the Quality Management System.


### 1.2 Scope
This procedure applies to all audit activities, including internal audits, supplier audits, and preparation for notified body and regulatory audits. This procedure does not cover detailed audit execution techniques (see ISO 19011 for guidance).
