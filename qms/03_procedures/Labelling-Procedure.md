# Labelling Procedure

## 1. Introduction
### 1.1 Purpose
This procedure defines the requirements and processes for developing, controlling, and maintaining product labeling and instructions for use (IFU).


### 1.2 Scope
This procedure applies to all labeling elements, including labels, packaging, instructions for use, and electronic IFU (eIFU). This procedure covers labeling content requirements, review, approval, and updates. Marketing materials are outside the scope of this procedure.
