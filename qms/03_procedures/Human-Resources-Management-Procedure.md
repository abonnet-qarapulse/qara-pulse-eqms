# Human Resources Management Procedure

## 1. Introduction
### 1.1 Purpose
This procedure defines the requirements and processes for managing human resources, including competence, training, and awareness of QMS requirements.


### 1.2 Scope
This procedure applies to all personnel whose work affects product quality and QMS effectiveness. This procedure covers recruitment, training, competence assessment, and awareness activities. General HR policies (compensation, benefits) are outside the scope of this procedure.
