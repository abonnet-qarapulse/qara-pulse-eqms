# Post-Market Surveillance Procedure

## 1. Introduction
### 1.1 Purpose
This procedure defines the requirements and process for systematic collection, analysis, and evaluation of post-market data to monitor device safety and performance.


### 1.2 Scope
This procedure applies to all post-market surveillance activities required by MDR, including PMS plans, PMS reports, and continuous monitoring. Vigilance activities (serious incident reporting) are covered by the Vigilance Procedure.
