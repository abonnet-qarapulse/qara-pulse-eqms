# Management Review Procedure

## 1. Introduction
### 1.1 Purpose
This procedure defines the requirements and process for conducting management reviews to ensure the continuing suitability, adequacy, and effectiveness of the QMS.


### 1.2 Scope
This procedure applies to periodic management reviews conducted by top management. This procedure defines the inputs, outputs, and review frequency. Day-to-day operational reviews are outside the scope of this procedure.
