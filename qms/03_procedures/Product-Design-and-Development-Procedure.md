## 1. Introduction
### 1.1 Purpose
This procedure defines the requirements and process for planning, executing, and controlling design and development activities for medical devices.

This procedure applies to all design and development activities, from design input to design transfer. This procedure covers design planning, reviews, verification, validation, and design changes. Software development specifics are detailed in the SDLC Procedure.
