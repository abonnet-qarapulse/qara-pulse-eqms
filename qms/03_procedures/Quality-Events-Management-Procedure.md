# Quality Events Management Procedure

## 1. Introduction
### 1.1 Purpose
This procedure defines the requirements and process for managing quality events, including nonconformities, corrective actions, and preventive actions (CAPA).


### 1.2 Scope
This procedure applies to all quality events, including nonconformities, deviations, and opportunities for improvement. This procedure covers event identification, investigation, root cause analysis, corrective actions, and preventive actions. Customer complaints are handled according to the Customer Support Procedure.
