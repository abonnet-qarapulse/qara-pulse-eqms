# List of Applicable Documents

## Purpose

This document identifies the **regulations, standards, and guidance documents** applicable to the development, maintenance, and lifecycle management of Software as a Medical Device (SaMD) and Artificial Intelligence as a Medical Device (AIaMD).

It serves as the **regulatory reference baseline** for the Quality Management System (QMS) and supports:
- regulatory strategy definition,
- QMS implementation,
- technical documentation preparation,
- audit and inspection activities.

---

## Scope

This list applies to:
- Software as a Medical Device (SaMD)
- AI as a Medical Device (AIaMD)
- Digital health products developed under the European Union regulatory framework

The applicability of each document depends on the **intended purpose, risk classification, technology used, and regulatory pathway** of the product.

---

## Applicable Regulations

| Regulation | Reference | Applicability | Justification |
|-----------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| EU Medical Device Regulation (MDR) | Regulation (EU) 2017/745 | Applicable | Software intended for a medical purpose qualifies as a medical device under MDR Article 2 |
| General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) | Regulation (EU) 2016/679 | Applicable | Processing of personal and health-related data |
| EU Artificial Intelligence Act (AI Act) | Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 | Applicable (when enforced) | AI systems used in medical contexts may qualify as high-risk |
| Electronic Instructions for Use (eIFU) | Regulation (EU) 2021/2226 | Applicable | Use of electronic instructions for use for medical devices |
